Empty Eye

Energy Conservation System

Empty Eye is an intelligent monitoring system that helps conserve energy by detecting when electrical appliances are left on in empty rooms. The system uses computer vision to detect human presence, running appliances like AC and fans, and even forgotten mobile devices.

When the system detects energy waste, it automatically sends email notifications to alert users, helping to save energy and reduce costs.
